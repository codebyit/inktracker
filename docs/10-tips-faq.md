# 10. Tips & FAQ

A few handy tips, a plain-English glossary, and answers to common questions.

---

## Handy tips
- **Print a worksheet** for the shop floor from a saved project (settings, ink, and
  materials on one page).
- **On mobile**, use the **bottom tab bar** for Dashboard, Projects, Service, Analytics,
  and Settings; tap the **hamburger (☰)** for everything else.
- **Install the app** for full-screen use and offline access — see
  [Getting Started](01-getting-started.md).
- **Lost connection?** An amber banner appears; pages you've opened keep working and it
  reconnects automatically.

## Glossary
| Term | Plain meaning |
|---|---|
| **COGS** | Cost of Goods Sold — total cost to make one item |
| **BOM** | Bill of Materials — the list of materials used in a job |
| **Margin** | Profit as a percentage of the sell price |
| **Channel** | One ink color (C, M, Y, K, W, GL, FW) |
| **Craft face** | One printed surface of a multi-part project |
| **Overhead** | A % added to cover rent, power, and other running costs |
| **PWA** | The app you can "install" from your browser |

## FAQ

**My profit numbers look wrong — why?**
Check **Settings**: ink costs, labor rate, and overhead drive every calculation.

**An ink level seems off.**
Log replacements in **Service**, or use an **ink correction** to adjust (you can undo).

**How do I free up my projects list?**
**Archive** finished jobs — they're hidden but never lost.

**Can I get my data out?**
Yes — **export projects to CSV**, download an **inventory PDF**, and take a **backup**
from Settings.

**It's not asking me to install on iPhone.**
iPhone doesn't auto-prompt — tap **Share → Add to Home Screen**.
